Course Units

Each unit combines micro-lectures, interactive prompting exercises, real-world case studies, and reflection opportunities designed for busy faculty. Learn at your own pace.

What You'll Master

By completing this micro-course, you'll gain concrete frameworks and hands-on experience to:

Define & Explain

Articulate what AI is, how it works, and its relevance to distance education in healthcare contexts with precision and clarity.

Apply Techniques

Write effective prompts for lesson planning, feedback generation, and case study development grounded in healthcare education.

Evaluate Critically

Recognize algorithmic bias, assess ethical implications, and make informed decisions about AI use in clinical teaching environments.

Design Activities

Create distance education learning activities that integrate AI as an augmentation tool, not a replacement for clinical judgment.

Build Assessments

Develop rubrics, feedback frameworks, and assessments that leverage AI while maintaining rigor and human-centered evaluation.
