AI Literacy in Healthcare DE

A theory-grounded micro-course for health science faculty teaching in distance education. Five units. Three hours. Rise 360 delivery.

  1. Unit 01 — Foundations of AI in Healthcare
    Explore what AI is, how it works in clinical contexts, and key terminology for educators.
    30 min
  2. Unit 02 — Prompt Engineering
    Master structured prompting techniques. Hands-on practice with healthcare use cases.
    40 min
  3. Unit 03 — AI Ethics and Bias in Clinical Contexts
    Examine algorithmic bias, disclosure ethics, and responsible AI implementation in healthcare education.
    35 min
  4. Unit 04 — Designing AI-Enhanced DE Activities
    Build distance education activities that integrate AI tools meaningfully without replacing clinical judgment.
    40 min
  5. Unit 05 — Assessment and Feedback with AI Tools
    Design rubrics, feedback frameworks, and assessments that leverage AI while maintaining rigor.
    35 min
