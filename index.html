What You'll Be Able to Do

By completing this micro-course, you will have concrete frameworks and hands-on experience to:

Define & Explain

Articulate what AI is, how it works, and its relevance to distance education in healthcare contexts.

Apply Techniques

Write effective prompts for lesson planning, feedback generation, and case study development.

Evaluate Critically

Recognize algorithmic bias, assess ethical implications, and make informed decisions about AI use in teaching.

Design Activities

Create distance education learning activities that integrate AI as an augmentation tool, not a replacement.

Build Assessments

Develop rubrics and feedback structures that use AI to enhance efficiency while maintaining educational rigor.
